Doxorubicin (DOX) Profile

Mechanism: DNA intercalation, topoisomerase II inhibition, ROS generation (Carvalho et al., 2009).

Side Effects:

  • Cardiotoxicity: Dose-dependent, higher in females/young (Lipshultz et al., 1995).
  • GI: Nausea (80%), vomiting (Griffin et al., 1996).
  • Hematologic: Neutropenia (MacDonald, 2009).
  • Other: Hair loss, hepatotoxicity, nephrotoxicity (Ajaykumar, undated).

Formulations: Conventional, Pegylated Liposomal (PLD), Non-Pegylated (Rivankar, 2014).
